History

LaFerrari


LaFerrari was officially presented to the world at The International Geneva Motor Show in March 2013.
It is a limited production hybrid sports car built by Italian car manufacturer Ferrari.
Translated as 'The Ferrari', it is considered to be the quintessential of the ultimate values and qualities of its luxury sports car manufacturer.

Characteristics and Design

With its mild hybrid engine, it provides the highest power output of any other Ferrari car, while decreasing fuel consumption by 40%.
It can go from 0-100 km/h in less than 3 seconds, 0-200 km/h in less than 7 seconds and 0-300 km/h in 15 seconds.
The maximum speed is over 350 km/h. This makes it the fastest road-car Ferrari ever produced.

The car's design remains essential and elegant in its shapes, which are meant to convey the high level of performance of the car.
